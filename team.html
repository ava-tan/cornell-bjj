Meet the Team:

The Cornell Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Club is dedicated to teaching a grappling-based martial art and is open to all. We have a vast number of members from all different kinds of backgrounds and skill levels. We have two core officers by the names of Micaela Carroll (depicted in the first picture posing) and Travis Fristoe (depicted in the blue in the second picture). The instructor is Tony Greenberg.
